Application for Admission for SY 2024-2025

The Technological University of the Philippines - Taguig is now accepting applications for the upcoming school year. Please follow the steps below to complete your application process.

Procedures for Online Application

It is recommended that the applicant use a laptop or desktop computer for online applications.

STEP 1: ERS WEBSITE

Visit the website: www.ers.tup.edu.ph/aims/applicants

A. Fill up the details:
  • Name: Dela Cruz
  • First Name: Juan
  • Middle Name: (Put NA if not applicable)
  • Birthdate: (set your mm-dd-yyyy)
  • Campus: Taguig
  • Department: College
B. Complete STEPS 1-4:
  • Complete all required fields. If not applicable, put NA.
  • Upload a picture. Rename your picture using your surname (e.g., Delacruz.jpg) and follow the guidelines carefully.
C. Download Form:
  • Download your Form A1 from ERS.
  • Ensure your photo is formal.
  • Get the Official Applicant ID for TUPTOAA purposes. DO NOT change your password yet.

Additional Information:

Please check your Official Applicant ID carefully. Do not change your password yet.

STEP 2: TUPTOAA

Complete the TUPTOAA form via Google Form - Click here

Provide the necessary details. Any false information is subject to disqualification.

STEP 3: SUBMIT THE REQUIREMENTS ON-SITE

Print the application form (TUP Form 1A) and the completed TUPTOAA acknowledgment (First page ONLY) from your personal email.

Attach the following documents:

  • For SHS applicants: Photocopy of Grade 11 and Grade 12 Card (Updated)
  • For Transferees: Copy of Transcript of Records or True Copy of Grades from the last school attended

Submit the above documents to the Registration and Admission Office at TUPT-Taguig, from FEBRUARY 5, 2024, to MAY 24, 2024, or until all slots are filled.

Important Note: Incomplete requirements will not be entertained.

Don't forget to submit your requirements within the deadline!

Paano makapasok sa TUP-Taguig?

Sa mga nais makapasok at makapag-aral sa TUP-Taguig, Ang TUP Taguig ay may entrance exam kung saan sinusukat ang kakayahan ng aplikante kung ito ay karapat-dapat sa napiling kurso. Magpasa ng mga dokumentong kailangan (Tignan ito).

Ang pagtanggap ng aplikante ay sa pagsisimula ng taon ng paaralan (every School-Year) lamang.

Kailan magbubukas ang TUP-Taguig sa Admission ngayong School-Year?

Ang TUP Taguig ay tumatanggap na ngayon ng mga aplikante para sa SY 2024-2025. Ang pagpapasa ng mga dokumento ay On-Site sa office ng Registration and Admission sa TUP-Taguig mula Pebrero 5, 2024 - Mayo 24, 2024 nang 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM, o hanggang lahat ng kurso ay mapuno.

Maari bang magtransfer ang studyante sa TUP-Taguig?

Ang TUP Taguig ay may polisiya para sa mga aplikante na mayroong college background (up to 2nd Year College). Kung ang aplikante ay walang bagsak sa dating pinasukang eskwelahan (University/College) at nakapagtapos ng SHS, ito ay maaaring lumipat sa nasabing unibersidad. Kung hindi man, ito ay inaabisuhang humanap ng iba pang unibersidad o kolehiyong papasukan.

Maari lamang na magpakita ng patunay (Transcript of Records) na ikaw ay walang bagsak sa naturang eskwelahan na pinasukan. May tatlong kursong programa ang maaring pasukan:

  • BET major in Automotive Technology
  • BET major in Dies and Moulds Technology
  • BET Major in Heating, Ventilation, and Airconditioning/Refrigeration Technology

May Tuition/ Miscellaneous Fees bang binabayaran sa TUP-Taguig at magkano?

Ang Tuition/Miscellaneous Fees sa TUP-Taguig ay subsidized ng gobyerno through RA 10931, o kilala bilang "Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act." Ang batas na ito ay nagtataguyod ng unibersal na pag-access sa de-kalidad na edukasyong tersiyaryo sa pamamagitan ng pagbibigay ng libreng matrikula at iba pang bayad sa paaralan sa mga Unibersidad at Kolehiyo ng Estado, Lokal na Unibersidad at Kolehiyo, at mga Institusyong Teknikal-Vokasyonal na Pinatatakbo ng Estado, pagtatatag ng Tertiary Education Subsidy at Student Loan Program, pagpapalakas ng Unified Student Financial Assistance System para sa Tertiary Education, at paglalaan ng pondo samakatuwid,‚Äù gaya ng nakasaad sa pamagat ng Batas.
