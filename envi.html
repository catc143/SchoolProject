Key Features of Our Environmental Science Courses

Our Environmental Science courses provide a comprehensive understanding of the natural world and the challenges we face in preserving it. We equip you with the knowledge and skills to drive sustainability and environmental protection.

Expert Faculty

Our faculty consists of experienced environmental scientists and sustainability experts who bring real-world knowledge into the classroom. Learn from specialists in ecology, environmental policy, and conservation science.

State-of-the-Art Labs

Our labs are equipped with advanced tools and technologies for environmental testing, including water quality analysis, soil testing, and climate monitoring. You'll gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge equipment in the field of environmental science.

Quality Education

Our curriculum covers key areas of environmental science, including environmental chemistry, ecosystem management, sustainability, and climate change. We provide you with the knowledge to address global environmental challenges and contribute to a sustainable future.

The Vision of TUP Taguig is to provide quality education that fosters innovation, creativity, and academic excellence, empowering students to contribute effectively to society and the global community.
