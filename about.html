Our History

The Technological University of the Philippines (TUP) was established in 1901 as the Manila Trade School, aiming to train youth in practical arts. Over the years, it evolved into a university offering a wide range of technical, engineering, and industrial programs. TUP is a pioneer in technological education in the Philippines.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To provide higher and advanced education in technological and engineering fields. TUP strives to produce globally competitive graduates who contribute to nation-building and the advancement of technology.

Our Vision

TUP envisions itself as a premier state university with recognized excellence in engineering, technology, and applied sciences.

Core Values

    Commitment to Excellence
    Innovative Thinking
    Integrity and Transparency
    Responsiveness to Society
